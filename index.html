Heat Stress Pilot Study

Guidelines and Instructions for Participants

Experiment Set-Up

1. Fitbit Sense 2 Smartwatch

  • Download the Fitbit app from the App Store (iOS) or Google Play Store (Android).
  • Create or log into your Fitbit account in the app.
  • Turn on your Fitbit Sense 2 and follow the in-app pairing instructions.
  • Allow permissions for heart rate, EDA, temperature, and sleep tracking.

Video for Settings Set-Up

  • Sleep and Skin Temperature
    • Go to the Today tab in the Fitbit app.
    • Tap the pencil/edit icon to customize visible cards.
    • Turn on "Sleep" and "Body Temperature" cards.
    • Wear the device overnight for at least 3 nights to collect data.
  • EDA and Stress Tracking
    • Go to Settings > Stress & Mindfulness in the app.
    • Turn on "Track body responses."
    • Leave "Body response notifications" off if not needed.
  • Heart Rate and HRV
    • Ensure the Heart Rate card is visible on the Today tab.
    • HRV is measured automatically overnight.
  • System Permissions
    • Enable Bluetooth, motion & fitness data, and background app refresh or physical activity access.
  • Participant Instructions
    • Wear the Fitbit snugly on your wrist during sleep for at least 3 nights.
    • Avoid removing the device unless necessary.
    • Open the Fitbit app daily to sync data.

2. GoveeLife Smart Air Quality Monitor

  • Download the Govee Home app from the App Store or Google Play Store.
  • Create or log into your Govee account.
  • Power on the air monitor and ensure Bluetooth is enabled on your phone.
  • Use the app to find and connect to the device, then follow prompts to finalize setup.

3. Braun ThermoScan 7 Connect

  • Download the Braun Health App from the App Store or Google Play Store.
  • Create or log into your Braun account.
  • Turn on the thermometer and enable Bluetooth on your phone.
  • Follow the app instructions to sync and store temperature readings.
