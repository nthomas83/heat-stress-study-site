Heat Stress Pilot Study

Guidelines and Instructions for Participants

Background

This study explores how working in high-heat environments affects individuals and aims to develop strategies to reduce heat-related health risks and improve safety practices for exposed workers.

Experiment Set-Up

  • Wear the provided Fitbit Sense 2 watch.
  • Log your activities using the Fitbit app.
  • Use a tympanic ear thermometer to take your temperature.
  • Monitor environmental conditions using the GoveeLife Air Quality Monitor.
  • Complete structured surveys after each session and at the end of the day.
