Equipment Set-Up

Fitbit Sense 2 Setup and Data Collection Instructions

1. Initial Setup

Follow the steps below to pair your Fitbit Sense 2 and enable required permissions.

ðŸ“¹ Fitbit Setup Video:

  • Download the Fitbit app from the App Store (iOS) or Google Play Store (Android).
  • Create a Fitbit account or log into an existing one.
  • Turn on your Fitbit Sense 2 and follow the in-app instructions to pair it with your phone.
  • Allow all necessary permissions for:
    • Heart Rate
    • EDA (electrodermal activity)
    • Skin Temperature
    • Sleep Tracking

2. Enable Required Settings

These settings must be turned on to ensure accurate data collection.

ðŸ“¹ Settings Set-Up Video:

  • Sleep & Skin Temperature
    • Open the Fitbit app and go to the Today tab.
    • Tap the pencil/edit icon to customize visible cards.
    • Make sure "Sleep" and "Body Temperature" cards are turned on.
    • Wear the device overnight for at least 3 nights to establish a temperature baseline.
  • EDA & Stress Tracking
    • Navigate to Settings > Stress & Mindfulness in the app.
    • Turn on "Track body responses."
    • You can keep "Body response notifications" off if not needed.
  • Heart Rate & HRV
    • Confirm the Heart Rate card is visible on the Today tab.
    • HRV is tracked automatically overnight when worn during sleep.
  • System Permissions
    • On your phone, ensure the following are enabled:
    • Bluetooth
    • Motion & Fitness Data
    • Background App Refresh (iOS) or Physical Activity Access (Android)
